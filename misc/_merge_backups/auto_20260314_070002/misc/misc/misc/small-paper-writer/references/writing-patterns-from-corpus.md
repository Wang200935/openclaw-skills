# Writing Patterns from Corpus

Use this reference when drafting in a style closer to real Taiwanese high-school information-category small papers. These patterns are derived from a corpus review of 404 papers and focus on what repeatedly appears in actual student writing.

## What the corpus sounds like
The dominant voice is not journal style.
It is a competition-paper style with student-report habits:
- fixed formal structure
- practical explanations
- moderate formality
- visible student perspective
- conclusions that stress feasibility, not grand theory

## Common macro structure
Most papers follow this skeleton:
- 前言
  - 研究動機
  - 研究目的
- 文獻探討
- 研究方法
- 研究分析與結果
- 研究結論與建議
- 參考文獻

Useful implication:
- keep the outer structure stable
- put individuality into motivation, method detail, and result interpretation

## Common opening pattern
The opening usually moves in this order:
1. background or trend
2. concrete observation
3. problem noticed by the student team
4. research purpose

Typical sentence moves:
- 近年來，______已成為______的重要議題。
- 在日常生活中，我們發現______。
- 某次在______的過程中，我們注意到______。
- 這讓我們開始思考______。
- 因此，本研究希望______。

Best practice from the corpus:
- keep the first background part short
- make the observation and trigger more specific than the trend statement

## Motivation patterns that feel real
Strong student-like motivations often come from:
- family care problems
- campus inconvenience
- classroom exposure to a tool
- club or elective project experience
- personal curiosity after seeing a news case
- repeated frustration during daily use of an app/system

This feels more natural than:
- humanity-level technological change
- very broad social transformation claims

## Research purpose patterns
Purpose sections are often listed and use verb-led items.

Common verbs:
- 了解
- 探討
- 分析
- 比較
- 建立
- 設計
- 開發
- 實作
- 驗證
- 提出建議

Common pattern:
- （一）了解______
- （二）比較______
- （三）設計並實作______
- （四）分析結果並提出建議

Corpus lesson:
- purposes are usually practical and bounded
- good papers do not pretend to answer an oversized academic question with a tiny method

## Literature review patterns
Most literature sections are not true scholarly debates.
They function as organized background for the later method.

Common internal organization:
- concept definition
- category breakdown
- tool/platform introduction
- prior application examples
- method comparison

Typical sentence patterns:
- 所謂______，是指______。
- 根據______指出，______。
- 文獻（作者，年份）提到______。
- ______可分為以下幾種。
- 本研究將相關內容整理如下。
- 由此可知______。

Best corpus-derived move:
end each literature chunk with a bridge sentence such as:
- 因此，本研究選擇______。
- 綜合上述文獻可知______，故本研究將聚焦於______。

## Method patterns
Method sections are usually procedural and detail-heavy. This is one of the strongest student-authenticity signals.

Common subsections:
- 研究流程
- 研究方法
- 研究工具 / 軟硬體設備
- 研究對象 / 問卷設計 / 系統架構
- 程式流程或模組說明

Typical sentence patterns:
- 本研究採用______法。
- 研究流程如下。
- 首先______，接著______，最後______。
- 使用______作為開發環境。
- 透過______進行資料蒐集與分析。
- 再依據結果進行修正與優化。

High-value details frequently found in the corpus:
- Python / Arduino / Unity / Colab / VS Code / Roboflow
- questionnaire counts
- image counts
- train/validation split
- model names
- hardware constraints

## Result patterns
Results are usually figure-led and explanation-first.
The writing tends to:
1. point to a figure/table
2. describe what happened
3. make a limited interpretation

Typical sentence patterns:
- 由圖一可知______。
- 由表二可得______。
- 根據問卷結果顯示______。
- 整體表現以______最佳。
- 與原先預期相比，______。

Corpus lesson:
- results usually stay close to the actual chart or test output
- strong papers avoid jumping too fast into theory-heavy interpretation

## Conclusion patterns
Conclusion sections usually do three things:
1. answer the research purpose
2. summarize what was built, compared, or found
3. admit limitations and future improvement

Typical sentence patterns:
- 綜合本研究結果可知______。
- 本研究成功完成______。
- 初步顯示______具有一定可行性。
- 但仍受限於______。
- 未來可朝______方向改進。

## Transition patterns seen repeatedly
These transitions are plain but stable.

Background -> motivation
- 然而，______
- 但在______情況下，______
- 這樣的現象讓我們開始思考______
- 基於上述背景，______

Motivation -> purpose
- 因此，本研究旨在______
- 有鑑於此，本研究希望______
- 基於上述原因，本研究之目的如下______

Literature -> own study
- 綜合上述文獻可知______
- 因此，本研究將______
- 參考前述研究，本研究______

Method -> results
- 完成上述流程後，______
- 經由實作與測試後，______
- 根據研究結果顯示，______

Results -> conclusion
- 綜合以上結果，______
- 整體而言，______
- 由本研究可知，______
- 未來若能______，將可進一步______

## Voice patterns that feel human
The corpus often sounds credible because of these traits:
- formal but not stiff
- willing to say 我們 / 本組 / 本研究
- careful claims instead of absolute claims
- visible learning-through-doing voice
- honest mention of limits

Useful phrases:
- 具有一定可行性
- 有助於______
- 可作為後續研究參考
- 期望能______
- 仍需進一步驗證
- 在實作過程中，我們發現______

## Signals that make writing sound too AI-generic
The corpus review repeatedly warns against these patterns:
- too much broad background unrelated to the actual topic
- encyclopedia-like paragraph stacking
- abstract praise words without scene or data
- conclusions that only compliment the study
- perfectly even paragraph rhythm with no rough edges

High-risk vague phrases:
- 提升效率
- 優化體驗
- 創新應用
- 重要意義
- 深遠影響
- 無限可能

Use them only if immediately followed by a specific object, user, or scenario.

## Corpus-derived drafting rules
- start from a real observed problem, not just a huge trend
- keep the contest structure conventional
- make literature support the method
- make method specific to tools, steps, and quantities
- state results as feasibility or observed difference
- admit limitations directly
- preserve a small but real student viewpoint

## Fast pattern selection guide
Use these defaults when unsure:
- Motivation: personal or campus trigger + why this problem matters
- Purpose: 3-4 listed verbs, each matched to later sections
- Literature: definition -> comparison -> why this matters for this paper
- Method: tools + flow + quantities + evaluation
- Results: figure/table description -> short interpretation
- Conclusion: answer purpose -> limit -> future direction
