# Page Budget

Use page budgeting before writing long drafts.

## Suggested allocation
- Abstract + keywords: 0.5 page
- Motivation / introduction: 0.5-1 page
- Literature review: 1-2 pages
- Method: 1-2 pages
- Findings / analysis: 1-3 pages
- Conclusion: 0.5-1 page
- References: as needed

## Rule
If the page limit is tight, cut literature repetition before cutting method or results.
