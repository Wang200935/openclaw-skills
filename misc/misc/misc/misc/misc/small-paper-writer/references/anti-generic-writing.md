# Anti-Generic Writing

Use this when the draft sounds too AI-generated or too smooth.

## Rewrite tactics
- Replace vague words with dataset-specific nouns
- Replace ?this shows? with the actual result
- Add realistic limits and uncertainty
- Use uneven sentence rhythm; mix short and medium sentences
- Remove empty praise words and inflated transitions
- Keep a student-researcher voice: serious, but not fake-professorial
