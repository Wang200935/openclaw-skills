# Writing Patterns from Corpus

Use this reference when drafting in a style closer to real Taiwanese high-school information-category small papers. These patterns are derived from a corpus review of 404 papers and focus on what repeatedly appears in actual student writing.

## What the corpus sounds like
The dominant voice is not journal style.
It is a competition-paper style with student-report habits:
- fixed formal structure
- practical explanations
- moderate formality
- visible student perspective
- conclusions that stress feasibility, not grand theory

Strong student-like motivations often come from:

- campus inconvenience
- classroom exposure to a tool
- club or elective project experience
- personal curiosity after seeing a news case

This feels more natural than:
- humanity-level technological change
- very broad social transformation claims
