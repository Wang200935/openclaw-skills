# Literature Review Guide

## Goal
Show what others already found, where the gap is, and why your paper is worth doing.

## Minimum pattern
1. Define the topic area
2. Summarize 3-8 relevant sources or prior examples
3. Compare them, do not just list them
4. Point out the gap your study addresses

## Good moves
- Group sources by theme, method, or conclusion
- Explain how each source helps your study
- Distinguish external findings from your own argument

## Bad moves
- Copy abstract-like summaries one by one
- Use references that you cannot trace
- Say ?many scholars think? without naming any source
