# Public Text Data Sources

## Common sources
- Google Play reviews
- Public YouTube comments
- Public forum posts (where collection is lawful and proportionate)
- News headlines and article metadata
- Open government text datasets
- Public app/store descriptions

## Selection rules
- Prefer stable, accessible, documentable sources
- Keep collection scope reproducible
- Record keywords, dates, and filtering steps
- Avoid sources that are hard to cite or impossible to reproduce
